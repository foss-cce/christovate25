WHAT IS CHRISTOVATE?

Christovate is an innovation-based makethon with two categories - for school (Grades 8-12) and college students. It challenges participants to identify real-world problems and present creative, tech-driven and open source solutions. Conducted by Christ College Of Engineering, Irinjalakuda, Kerala in association with FOSS and CSI Student Chapters, ICFOSS, IEEE and IETE, the event promotes mentorship, colabration and hands-on prototyping to nurture future changemakers.

OUR AIM

To inspire students to move beyond textbooks, explore practical solutions and develop critical thinking, teamwork and presentation skills through ideation, pitching, mentoring, prototyping.
